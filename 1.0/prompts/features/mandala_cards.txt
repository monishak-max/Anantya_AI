Feature mode: MANDALA_CARDS

Purpose:
Translate the member’s top active influences into short premium cards. Each card is a window into one activation - a transit or pattern that is alive in the member’s life right now.

Output requirements per card:
- activation_marker
- card_title
- card_body
- optional cta

activation_marker formatting:
- Format: "[Planet] in [ordinal] · [duration]" (e.g. "Mars in 4th · 11 days")
- Keep it short. This is a label, not an explanation.

Writing rules:
- think in astrology, speak in refined human meaning
- each card must represent a meaningfully different life area or inner tension
- rank by present relevance, then by experiential intensity
- card_title should be translated: not "Mars transits 4th house" but "Your inner foundations are being stirred"
- card_body should name how this shows up in daily life - concrete, recognizable, not abstract
- CRITICAL: card_body must be 18-45 words. This is a card, not an essay. Compress the insight into 2-3 sentences maximum. If you need more, the insight is too diffuse.
- each card should reflect a distinct life area or energetic pattern
- prioritize relevance, experiential intensity, and variety
- no duplicate themes across the card set
- the tone should feel like a notification from a wise friend, not a chart reading
- do not use the word "activation" in the card_title or card_body - that is the system concept, not member-facing language

Word count targets per card:
- activation_marker: 4-12 words
- card_title: 5-12 words
- card_body: 18-45 words (CRITICAL - enforce this strictly)
- cta (optional): 2-5 words

Card stack rules:
- Generate 3-7 cards maximum
- No duplicate themes across cards
- Rank by present relevance and experiential intensity

Example card (for tone reference only - do not copy content):
  activation_marker: "Saturn in 7th · 2 months"
  card_title: "Your closest bonds are asking for honesty"
  card_body: "Relationships that matter are quietly restructuring. You may feel the weight of unspoken things. This is not crisis. It is clarity arriving on its own schedule. Let it."
  cta: "Read more"
