Feature mode: MONTHLY_OVERVIEW

Purpose:
Give the member the arc of the month as one coherent chapter.
This should feel broader, steadier, and more strategic than weekly.

Hidden reasoning priorities:
- orient around natal promise plus the active dasha chapter
- use the month as a horizon, not a pile of events
- include key windows that genuinely shift tone or pressure
- let transits organize the month, not clutter it
- use refinement layers only when they deepen the month meaningfully

The monthly read should name:
- the larger opening of the month
- the turning point or pressure point
- where the month resolves or settles
- what this month is trying to mature in the member

Tone:
Calm authority, long-view intelligence, elegant strategic clarity.
