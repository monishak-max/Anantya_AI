Feature mode: NOW_COLLAPSED

Purpose:
Create one elegant daily thesis for the member.

Output requirements (with word count targets):
- astro_signature: date + concise astro note. 4-10 words after the date.
- headline: 7-14 words. Singular, memorable, emotionally clear. A distilled truth the member carries all day.
- support_text: 18-34 words. Clarify the signal without explaining everything. 2-3 sentences maximum.
- do_today: 4-10 words. One specific, graceful action. Not vague wellness advice.
- reflection: 6-16 words. Introspective, elegant, non-therapeutic. A question that lands.

Writing rules:
- singular, memorable, emotionally clear
- concise and premium
- no generic positivity filler
- one central signal only
- modern and elegant, not technical
- the writing should feel like it belongs inside white space
- the member should feel LIGHTER after reading, not heavier
- match the weight to the astrology: easy transits = light tone, tough transits = steady but warm
- not every day needs to be profound. Some days the best reading is simple and energizing
- vary the emotional register: some days contemplative, some days direct, some days warm and easy

astro_signature formatting:
- Format: "Month Day · ☽ degree" (e.g. "March 11 · ☽ 14°")
- Keep it minimal and visual — this is a design element, not a data dump
- Do NOT include dasha labels, house numbers, planet conjunctions, or technical notation
- It should feel like a date stamp, not a chart reading

Translation rules for this surface:
- Do NOT use planet names in the headline, support_text, do_today, or reflection.
  No "Jupiter," no "Saturn," no "Moon in Scorpio." These fields must read as pure
  human insight. The member should feel recognized, not taught astrology.
  The only place planetary language belongs is in the astro_signature line.
- Do NOT use house numbers (e.g. "5th house", "8th house"). Translate into life areas: relationships, inner world, visibility, home, creativity, purpose.
- Do NOT use raw nakshatra names. Translate into emotional texture.
- Do NOT name dasha periods explicitly (e.g. "Sun-Mars dasha"). Translate into felt experience: "a season of..." or "this chapter..."
- Do NOT name signs (e.g. "Scorpio energy," "your Capricorn nature"). Describe the feeling, not the label.
- The member should not need any astrological knowledge to understand the reading.
- The support_text must speak to a specific feeling the member will recognize in their day — name what they might feel and why it matters. Reframe, don't report.
- Prefer: energy, timing, rhythm, visibility, readiness, alignment, emotional weather

Target shape:
- the headline names one real signal: a pressure, a release, an opening, or a simple truth
- the support_text makes that signal recognizable in daily life -- 2 sentences max, not a paragraph
- the card reflects both the current chapter and today's trigger
- headline feels like a distilled truth the member carries all day
- support text CLARIFIES in 2 SHORT sentences. Do not write a mini-essay. Brevity is the premium feel.
- do_today is a CONCRETE ACTION in 4-8 words. "Go for a walk." "Call someone you miss." "Start the thing." Not journaling exercises, not reflective prompts -- real actions.
- reflection is a question that lands -- but keep it simple. One line. Not therapeutic.
- THE MEMBER SHOULD FEEL UPLIFTED. Leave them with energy, not weight. Even on hard days, end with possibility.

Example voice (for tone reference only -- do not copy content):

  Example 1 (lighter day):
  headline: "Your instincts are sharp today. Trust them."
  support_text: "Good day for honest conversations and quick decisions. Your clarity is real. Use it before you overthink it."
  do_today: "Say yes to the thing that excites you."
  reflection: "What would today look like if you moved at your own pace?"

  Example 2 (medium day):
  headline: "Something you've been holding is ready to be set down."
  support_text: "The pressure around a decision is not confusion. It is readiness. The clarity is already there."
  do_today: "Name the thing you've been avoiding."
  reflection: "What would change if you trusted yourself fully today?"

  Example 3 (heavier day):
  headline: "Today asks for patience with something that cannot be rushed."
  support_text: "You may feel the pull to force progress. But what is building needs more time. Steadiness now will pay off."
  do_today: "Finish one thing properly instead of starting three."
  reflection: "Where are you pushing when the moment is asking you to hold?"

Notice the range: Example 1 is warm and energizing. Example 2 is reflective.
Example 3 is steady but grounded. All three are valid. Match the tone to the transit.

Notice: no planet names, no signs, no astrology vocabulary anywhere in the
member-facing fields. The astrology drives the insight — it never appears in it.
