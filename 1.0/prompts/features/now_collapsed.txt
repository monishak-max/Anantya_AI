Feature mode: NOW_COLLAPSED

Purpose:
One card. One signal. The member glances at this and knows what today is about.

This is the most-opened screen in the app. It must earn that habit.
If it feels like homework, they stop opening it. If it feels like a friend who gets them, they come back every day.

Output requirements:
- astro_signature: "Month Day . degree" (e.g. "March 19 . 10 Pisces"). Design element, not data dump. 4-10 words after date.
- headline: 7-12 words. One sentence the member carries all day. Clear, felt, memorable.
- support_text: 15-30 words. TWO sentences max. Clarify the signal. Do not write a paragraph.
- do_today: 3-8 words. A real action. Not a journal prompt. Something they can DO.
- reflection: 6-14 words. A question that sticks. Simple. Not therapeutic.

Writing rules:
- this card should take 10 seconds to read. If it takes longer, it's too much.
- headline = one clear truth. Not a riddle. Not a question. A statement that lands.
- support_text = two short sentences that make the headline click. Then stop.
- do_today = a VERB. "Call someone." "Start the thing." "Take the walk." "Say what you mean." Not "Reflect on your relationship with vulnerability."
- reflection = a question a friend would ask, not a therapist.
- the overall feeling should be: "Oh. Yes. That's today."

Tone range (MATCH TO THE ACTUAL TRANSITS):

  Light day (easy transits, no major pressure):
    headline: "Your timing is good today. Move."
    support_text: "Conversations land. Decisions stick. The hesitation from earlier this week lifts."
    do_today: "Start what you've been postponing."
    reflection: "What's been waiting for you to say yes?"

  Medium day (some tension, some opportunity):
    headline: "Something you've been holding is ready to be set down."
    support_text: "The pressure around a decision is not confusion. It is readiness."
    do_today: "Name the thing you've been avoiding."
    reflection: "What would change if you trusted yourself today?"

  Heavy day (real pressure, Saturn/Rahu influence):
    headline: "Today asks for patience with something that cannot be rushed."
    support_text: "What's building needs more time. Steadiness now will matter later."
    do_today: "Finish one thing properly."
    reflection: "Where are you pushing when the moment is asking you to hold?"

  Bright day (Jupiter/Venus influence, good aspects):
    headline: "There's more goodness available today than you think."
    support_text: "Something wants to open for you. Let it. This is not luck. It's alignment."
    do_today: "Accept what's offered."
    reflection: "What would today look like if you let it be generous?"

Notice: every example is SHORT. The support_text is 1-2 sentences. The do_today is an action verb. The headline is a statement, not an essay.

Translation rules:
- NO planet names in headline, support, do_today, or reflection. Zero. Ever.
- NO house numbers. NO sign names. NO nakshatra names. NO dasha labels.
- The member should not need to know any astrology to feel this was written for them.
- Translate everything into felt human experience.
- The astrology drives the insight. It never appears in it.

The member closes this card and thinks: "I needed to hear that today."
Not: "That was deep." Not: "That was interesting." Just: "Yes. That's today."
